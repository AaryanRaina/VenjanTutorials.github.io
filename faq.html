Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to Pay anything to enroll as a Teacher?
No. There is no registration fee to be paid to Venjan Tutorials for enrolling and getting listed as a Teacher.
How long does it take to get listed on Venjan Tutorials?
The process of getting listed on Venjan Tutorials is extremely simple and quick. You just need to clear an online assessment and/or provide a teaching demo to our experts. On an average the entire process can be completed within a week's time.
What subjects are offered at Venjan Tutorials?
A student can take up personalized online tuitions in Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, English, Hindi, Sanskrit, French, German, Social Studies and Computer Science at Venjan Tutorials. Apart from these core curricular subjects, they can also enroll for various exams.
What if I am not able to teach for the initially committed duration?
At Venjan Tutorials, you teach at your convenient time and/or at a time that is mutually convenient to you and your students. With the essential tools and features, you can adjust your availability to teach on Venjan Tutorials anytime and be visible to a pool of students.
What are the basic requisites to conduct online tutoring sessions at Venjan Tutorials?
As far as hardware is concerned, a laptop/desktop with good processing power, minimum of 4 GB RAM, broadband Internet connectivity (atleast 4 mbps) is required along with a digital pen tablet or stylus. Power backup definitely is an add-on in case your area has electricity issues. Rest, our Academic team takes care of all the training you need to kickstart your sessions on Venjan Tutorials.
How much time do I need to spare to conduct online tutoring sessions?
Venjan Tutorials is a highly flexible platform that will enable you to teach whenever you can. It all depends on your availability to teach online. Our teachers spend 3-4 hours per day on an average. However, a commitment of at least 2 hours per day between 12pm and 8 pm is a requisite..
Is computer proficiency a must to conduct these online sessions?
Yes, It is. Our Teacher Care team also conducts intensive trainings to help you deliver online sessions better at Venjan Tutorials.
Will I be given any training to conduct these online sessions?
Yes, trainings are integral part of the on-boarding process for every new teacher at Venjan Tutorials. Our Teacher Care team is always available for helping teachers with anything and everything they need.
Will I receive technical support if I face any issues during my sessions?
Definitely. We at Venjan Tutorials are extremely sensitive about the wholesome experience of learning and teaching. Hence, our Teacher Care and Tech teams are always available for immediate resolutions to any issues you face.
Do I need to Sign any contract or bonds?
As we said, Venjan Tutorials is a highly flexible platform. You teach at your convenient time, and hence you do not have to sign any contract or bond with us.
What are my responsibilities at Venjan Tutorials as a Teacher?
We expect all our teachers to act as an end-to-end learning solution providers for the students. In simple words, the teacher understands the learning requirement of the student, creates the learning plan for the student, prepares the relevant content required for the sessions, shares tests and assignments, evaluates them to understand student progress and provides regular feedback on the same to students and parents.
Is computer proficiency a must to conduct these online sessions?
Yes, It is. Our Teacher Care team also conducts intensive trainings to help you deliver online sessions better at Venjan Tutorials.
